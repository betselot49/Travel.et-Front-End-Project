Ethiopian airways

Ethiopian Airlines is the national flag carrier of Ethiopia and one of the largest airlines in Africa. It is owned by the Ethiopian government and is headquartered at Bole International Airport in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. The airline operates flights to more than 104 destinations across five continents, making it one of the most extensive networks on the continent. In addition to its regular routes, Ethiopian Airlines also provides charter services. Established in 1945.
